üêõ Dead Bug Progression

Position: Lying on the back (supine)
Primary Focus: Preventing the lower back from arching away from the floor
Repetitions: 5x each level
Level 1 5 reps each side
Starting Position: Knees bent, feet flat on floor
Movement: Raised right foot 5x, then left foot 5x (lifting foot a few inches off the floor or extending knee slightly)
Primary Challenge: Spine Stability - Maintaining the initial spinal position while lifting one foot.
Level 2 5 reps each side
Starting Position: Knees bent, feet flat on floor
Movement: Raised right hand together with right foot 5x (same side limb movement)
Primary Challenge: Coordinated Unilateral Movement - Coordinating same-side arm and leg movement while maintaining spinal position.
Level 3 5 reps alternating
Starting Position: Knees bent, feet flat on floor
Movement: Contralateral movements - Right hand with left foot 5x, then left hand with right foot 5x
Primary Challenge: Contralateral Stability - Stabilizing the core while moving limbs on opposite sides of the body.
Level 4 5 reps alternating
Starting Position: Both hands raised, both feet raised (table-top position)
Movement: Slowly placed right hand on floor with left foot 5x, then left hand with right foot 5x
Primary Challenge: High-Level Contralateral Stability - Moving opposite limbs from elevated, unstable starting position requiring maximum core control.

ü¶Ö Bird Dog Progression

Position: On hands and knees (quadruped)
Primary Focus: Maintaining flat (neutral) back and keeping hips level
Repetitions: 5x each level
Level 1 5 reps
Starting Position: Hands and knees on floor
Movement: Standard Bird Dog - Raised right hand and left foot together 5x (opposite arm and leg extension)
Primary Challenge: Spinal Anti-Rotation - Stabilizing the core to prevent trunk twisting as limbs extend.
Level 2 5 reps
Starting Position: Extended Bird Dog position
Movement: Brought right hand to side & twisted body 5x (thoracic rotation/crunch while balancing on left hand and right knee)
Primary Challenge: Rotational Stability & Mobility - Maintaining balance and hip stability while actively rotating the upper body.
